Description

B2Gold Corp is a gold mining company, with five operating mines and numerous exploration projects across four continents in various countries, including Nicaragua, the Philippines, Namibia, Mali, and Burkina Faso. The company's reportable operating segments include its mining operations and development projects, namely the Limon, Libertad, Masbate, and Otjikoto mines, and the Fekola, Gramalote, and Kiaka projects.
